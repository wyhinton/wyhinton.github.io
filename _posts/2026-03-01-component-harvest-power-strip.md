I recently had a project that required an on-off toggle switch. While a pack of such switches only runs a couple of bucks on Ali express, I didn't really want to have a bunch of extra switches laying around. For this project I needed to control DC voltage with the switch. Turns out AC switches also work (within rated limits) for DC power, and then I realized that your every day power strips contain such switches. I see these at the flea market for $1 or $2. As an added bonus you get some decently thick copper bus bars, and in this case, a couple of screws.
